## Research

### Trespass Towing in Montgomery County

In partnership with Montgomery County, research on trespass towing data led to four web apps that address common parking and towing challenges. The apps map tow hotspots, highlight companies with frequent violations, and simplify complaint reporting, translating detailed county data into practical, user-facing solutions.

https://a1-3x.github.io/tow_analysis/

#### TowR

The Towr app is aimed at consumers. Its purpose is to prevent trespass tows from occuring by educating vehicle owners on where vehicles have been towed from in the past. Consumers looking to park their vehicle may look up an address, or share their location with the app, and will be met with a map indicating locations nearby that have seen trespass tows in the past 3 years.

https://towr.netlify.app/

#### Tow Ranker

The Tow Ranking web app demonstrates the capability of this or similar data to rank merchants on their level of deviation from the mean. Applications based on different measures can be developed as their importance is uncovered. More information and calculations can be found [here](https://github.com/A1-3x/tow/blob/main/ranking-app.md).

https://towrank.netlify.app/


### Migration Between the United States

An interactive dashboard was created to explore 20 years of U.S. census migration data. Users can analyze patterns of interstate migration, while an accompanying heatmap visualizes the probability of moving from one state to another, turning complex population data into clear, interactive insights.
